Here are answers to a few frequently asked questions about intro to algorathims

How do i report errors?

First visit the erreta page to verify that the error has not been reported already. Once that you have determined that you've found an unreported error, send email at clrs-bugs@mit.edu.

Do you correct errors?

Yes, each time a new printing is produced, it contains corrections to all errors that have been reported by that time.
